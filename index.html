About Me

I'm Emmanuel Adenuga, a Business Analyst based in Darlington, UK, with a proven track record of bridging the gap between business needs and data-driven solutions. I specialise in requirements elicitation, stakeholder engagement, process mapping and analytics — helping organisations in healthcare, aviation, retail and FMCG make smarter, faster decisions.

My analytical toolkit spans Power BI, SQL, Microsoft Excel, Jira, Confluence and Lucidchart. Whether mapping AS-IS processes, building dashboards or facilitating stakeholder workshops, I deliver clear documentation and actionable recommendations that create real organisational impact.
